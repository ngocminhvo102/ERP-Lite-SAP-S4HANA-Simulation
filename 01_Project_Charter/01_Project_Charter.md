# Executive Summary

Minh Phat Distribution is a mid-sized distribution company operating in the consumer goods industry.

The company currently relies on multiple disconnected systems including Excel spreadsheets, Zalo communications, Email, and MISA Accounting Software to manage daily operations.

As the company continues to grow, operational inefficiencies have become increasingly apparent, including duplicate data entry, lack of real-time inventory visibility, delayed reporting, and limited process standardization.

The objective of this project is to design and implement an integrated ERP solution that centralizes business processes, improves operational efficiency, and provides management with real-time visibility into company performance.

# Business Problems

The organization currently experiences several operational challenges:

## Sales

* Quotation management is handled through Excel files.
* Inventory availability must be verified manually through warehouse personnel.
* Discount approvals are not consistently controlled.
* Sales order tracking is fragmented.

## Warehouse

* Inventory visibility is not available in real time.
* Batch and expiry date management are performed manually.
* Inventory transactions are recorded in spreadsheets.
* Stock reconciliation is time-consuming.

## Human Resources

* Employee records are maintained in Excel.
* Leave requests are processed through Zalo.
* Attendance data is maintained separately.
* HR reporting is manual.

## Finance

* Data is re-entered multiple times across systems.
* Financial reports are not available in real time.
* Accounts receivable tracking is partially manual.
* Management reporting is delayed.
