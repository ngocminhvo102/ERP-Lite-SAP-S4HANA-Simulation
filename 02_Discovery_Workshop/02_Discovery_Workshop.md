# Business Overview

Minh Phat Distribution is a consumer goods distribution company operating with:

* 50 Employees
* 2 Branches
* 2 Warehouses
* 2,000+ SKUs

The company currently relies on multiple disconnected systems such as Excel, Zalo, Email, and MISA Accounting Software.

Business growth has increased operational complexity, resulting in inefficient processes and limited management visibility.

# Discovery Questions

## Sales

### Questions Asked

* What products does the company sell?
* How are quotations currently created?
* How are customer discounts approved?
* How does Sales verify inventory availability?
* How are sales orders communicated to the warehouse?
* How does Accounting receive sales transaction information?
* How are customer receivables tracked?
* How does management monitor sales performance?

### Findings

* Quotations are prepared manually using Excel.
* Inventory availability is confirmed through phone calls or Zalo messages.
* Discount approvals are not standardized.
* Sales order tracking is fragmented.
* Data is entered multiple times across departments.

---

## Warehouse

### Questions Asked

* How many warehouses are currently operating?
* Is batch management required?
* Is expiry date tracking required?
* How are inventory movements recorded?
* How is inventory counted and reconciled?
* How does the warehouse communicate stock shortages?
* How are stock transfers managed?

### Findings

* Inventory is maintained in Excel.
* Stock availability is not visible in real time.
* Batch tracking is manual.
* Expiry date monitoring is manual.
* Inventory count requires significant effort.
* Stock discrepancies are difficult to investigate.

---

## Human Resources

### Questions Asked

* How are employee records maintained?
* How are leave requests managed?
* How is attendance recorded?
* How is employee information reported to management?
* How is payroll information prepared?

### Findings

* Employee information is maintained in Excel.
* Leave requests are submitted through Zalo.
* Attendance data is stored separately.
* HR reporting is mostly manual.

---

## Finance

### Questions Asked

* How are revenues recorded?
* How are expenses recorded?
* How are customer receivables monitored?
* How are supplier payables monitored?
* What reports are required by management?

### Findings

* Financial transactions are recorded in MISA.
* Data is manually transferred between departments.
* Financial reporting is not available in real time.
* Management reporting requires manual consolidation.

# Key Pain Points Identified

## Sales

* No real-time inventory visibility.
* Manual quotation process.
* Duplicate data entry.
* Limited order tracking.

## Warehouse

* No real-time stock information.
* Manual batch tracking.
* Manual expiry management.
* Time-consuming stock reconciliation.

## Human Resources

* Decentralized employee information.
* Manual leave management.
* Manual reporting process.

## Finance

* Duplicate data entry.
* Delayed reporting.
* Limited visibility of receivables and payables.
