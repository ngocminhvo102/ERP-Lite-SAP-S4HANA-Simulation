# AS-IS Process Analysis

## Module

Sales Management

---

# Business Objective

Manage customer quotations, sales orders, and product sales activities.

---

# Current Process Flow

Customer Inquiry

↓

Sales Creates Quotation in Excel

↓

Sales Contacts Warehouse for Inventory Check

↓

Warehouse Verifies Inventory in Excel

↓

Inventory Availability Confirmed

↓

Sales Sends Quotation to Customer

↓

Customer Approves

↓

Sales Creates Sales Order in Excel

↓

Sales Sends Order Information via Zalo

↓

Warehouse Processes Delivery

↓

Accounting Records Invoice in MISA

---

# Process Analysis

| Step                    | User              | Current Tool |
| ----------------------- | ----------------- | ------------ |
| Create Quotation        | Sales             | Excel        |
| Check Inventory         | Sales + Warehouse | Phone, Zalo  |
| Approve Quotation       | Customer          | Email, Zalo  |
| Create Sales Order      | Sales             | Excel        |
| Send Order to Warehouse | Sales             | Zalo         |
| Delivery Processing     | Warehouse         | Excel        |
| Invoice Recording       | Accounting        | MISA         |

# Pain Point Analysis

## Pain Point 1

### Description

Inventory availability is not visible in real time.

### Current Situation

Sales must contact Warehouse to verify stock.

### Impact

* Delayed customer response
* Risk of providing incorrect inventory information
* Reduced customer satisfaction

---

## Pain Point 2

### Description

Quotation management is manual.

### Current Situation

Sales creates quotations in Excel.

### Impact

* Inconsistent pricing
* Risk of calculation errors
* Difficult quotation tracking

---

## Pain Point 3

### Description

Data is entered multiple times.

### Current Situation

Sales, Warehouse, and Accounting maintain separate records.

### Impact

* Increased workload
* Duplicate effort
* Higher error rate

---

## Pain Point 4

### Description

Order status is difficult to track.

### Current Situation

No centralized order tracking system exists.

### Impact

* Limited visibility
* Delayed issue resolution
* Customer service challenges

# Root Cause Analysis

| Issue                 | Root Cause                      |
| --------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| Inventory uncertainty | No centralized inventory system |

| Reporting delays      | Lack of integration             |

# AS-IS Summary

The current sales process relies heavily on Excel files, phone calls, and Zalo communications.

The absence of an integrated ERP solution results in manual effort, duplicate data entry, delayed reporting, and limited visibility into operational performance.

The identified pain points will be addressed in the TO-BE process design phase.
