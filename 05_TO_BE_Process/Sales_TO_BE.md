# TO-BE Process Design

## Module

Sales Management

---

# Business Objective

Provide an integrated sales process that enables quotation management, order processing, inventory visibility, and invoicing through a centralized ERP system.

---

# Future Process Flow

Customer Inquiry

↓

Sales Creates Quotation in ERP

↓

ERP Validates Pricing

↓

Sales Submits Quotation

↓

Customer Approves

↓

Sales Creates Sales Order

↓

ERP Checks Inventory Availability

↓

Manager Approval (if required)

↓

Warehouse Receives Delivery Request

↓

Goods Delivered

↓

ERP Generates Invoice

↓

Accounting Receives Transaction Automatically

↓

Management Dashboard Updates in Real Time
