# TO-BE Process Design

## Module

Human Resource Management

---

# Business Objective

Provide centralized employee management, leave processing, attendance integration, and workforce visibility.

---

# Future Process Flow

HR Creates Employee Master

↓

Employee Assigned to Department

↓

Employee Check-In / Check-Out

↓

Attendance Data Integrated

↓

Employee Creates Leave Request

↓

Manager Approves Request

↓

Leave Balance Updates Automatically

↓

HR Dashboard Updates

↓

Management Views Workforce Information

| AS-IS                      | TO-BE                  |

| Manual Leave Balance       | Automatic Update       |
| Manual Attendance Tracking | Attendance Integration |
| Manual HR Reporting        | HR Dashboard           |

* Improved HR reporting
