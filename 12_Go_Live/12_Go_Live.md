# Go-Live Plan

## Project

ERP Lite SAP S/4HANA Simulation

## Objective

The objective of the Go-Live phase is to successfully transition business operations from legacy processes (Excel, Zalo, Manual Tracking) to the ERP Lite system.

The Go-Live Plan ensures business continuity, user readiness, data accuracy, and system stability.

---

# Go-Live Strategy

## Go-Live Approach

### Big Bang Approach

All departments will migrate to the ERP system on the same date.

Modules included:

- Sales
- Warehouse
- Human Resources
- Finance
- Dashboard Reporting
