The ERP Lite solution will centralize Sales, Inventory, Human Resources, and Finance processes into a single integrated platform.

Key objectives:

- Single Source of Truth
- Real-Time Visibility
- Standardized Processes
- Reduced Manual Work
- Improved Reporting
